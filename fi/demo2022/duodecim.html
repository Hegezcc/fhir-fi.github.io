Duodecim

Duodecim presents FINRISK calculator integrated with SMART app launch framework. The implementation has been done in collaboration with Solita. The FINRISK calculator has long been available through the Terveyskirjasto health library to the public. What is new is the capability to prefill calculators and forms using FHIR-based clinical data sources. This allows semi-automatic combination of existing medical data with medical content, such a FINRISK calculator, and eventually the creation of clinical indicators as FHIR resources.
